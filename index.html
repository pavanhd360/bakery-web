Our Story

LocalBacker began in 2010 with a simple mission: to bring authentic, artisanal baking to our community. Our founder, Emma, learned traditional baking techniques from her grandmother and combined them with modern flavors.

Today, we continue to bake everything by hand, using locally-sourced ingredients and traditional methods that have been passed down through generations.
